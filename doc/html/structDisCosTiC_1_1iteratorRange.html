Detailed Description

template<typename scalarT>
struct DisCosTiC::iteratorRange< scalarT >

iterator ranges for each entityTypes to support iteration with range-based for loops. Iterating over sets of entityTypes is one of the most common operation. Our infrastructure implements this custom range-based for loops in the C++ ways by providing iterators and matching begin(), end() and stepSize(scalarT stepSize) methods.
